Project: TeleCare

TeleCare is a telemedicine SaaS platform connecting patients and doctors.

Core features:

- Patient signup/login
- Doctor profiles
- Appointment booking
- Video consultations
- Secure messaging
- Medical record uploads
- Digital prescriptions

Users:

1. Patient
2. Doctor
3. Admin

Goal:
Build an MVP telemedicine product that allows patients to book doctors and have video consultations remotely.
