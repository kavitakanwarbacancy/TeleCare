## TeleCare Product Demo Script

Use this as a spoken script while you click through the demo. Sentences are short and simple on purpose.

---

### 1. Intro (landing / login screen)

“Access to healthcare should be simple, fast, and available from anywhere.  
But for many patients and providers, managing appointments, consultations, and medical records is still fragmented and time‑consuming.  

At Bacancy, we’ve spent over 14 years building healthcare software and digital care platforms for organizations around the world.  

TeleCare is one of our telemedicine platforms. It’s designed to enable secure virtual consultations, smooth workflows for providers, and better engagement for patients.  

In this demo, I’ll walk you through a typical virtual consultation journey using TeleCare, so you can see how the product works in real life.”

---

### 2. Patient login and dashboard

“Let’s start with the patient experience.  

Here, the patient can securely sign in to their account.  

Once logged in, they land on their personal dashboard. From here, they can quickly see their upcoming appointments, past consultations, and shortcuts to key areas like medical records and prescriptions.  

This makes it easy for patients to understand what’s coming next in their care, without needing to call the clinic.”

---

### 3. Doctor login and dashboard

“At the same time, doctors have their own dedicated dashboard.  

Here, the doctor can see their daily schedule, upcoming teleconsultations, and key information about each patient they’re going to meet.  

Before each session, the doctor can open the patient profile, quickly review past visits, notes, and any important history, so they’re fully prepared even before the call starts.”

_(On screen: show doctor dashboard and, if possible, have the patient dashboard visible in another window or cut between them.)_

---

### 4. Real‑time notifications and appointments

“Whenever a patient schedules or updates an appointment, both the patient and the doctor receive real‑time updates inside the platform.  

This helps reduce no‑shows, keeps everyone aligned on timings, and ensures consultations happen smoothly and on time.  

From the appointments view, the patient can easily see what’s booked, and the doctor sees the same appointment reflected in their schedule.”

---

### 5. Joining the teleconsultation

“When it’s time for the consultation, joining the call is very simple.  

The patient just clicks the ‘Join Call’ or ‘Start Consultation’ button from their appointment. With one click, they enter a secure video consultation from wherever they are.  

On the doctor’s side, they also click to join the same session directly from their schedule. The platform connects both sides in a secure, HIPAA‑friendly environment, with the patient’s context visible to the doctor.”

---

### 6. During the consultation: chat, notes, and prescription

“During the consultation, the doctor can talk to the patient over video, and also use built‑in chat if they want to send quick messages, links, or instructions.  

At the same time, the doctor can review symptoms, capture notes, and update the clinical information without leaving the call screen.  

Here, you can see the doctor adding their assessment and plan for this visit. They can also generate a digital prescription directly inside the platform, choosing medications, dosage, and instructions.  

All of this is saved to the patient’s record immediately, so nothing gets lost or written on paper.”

---

### 7. Ending the call and patient follow‑up

“Once the consultation is complete, the doctor ends the call, and the visit is automatically recorded as completed in their schedule.  

On the patient side, the consultation details are now available in their account.  

The patient can open the visit summary to see what was discussed, review their digital prescription, and follow any instructions shared by the doctor.  

If needed, they can also book a follow‑up consultation with the same doctor in just a few clicks, which supports continuous, long‑term care.”

---

### 8. Beyond consultations (high‑level capabilities)

“Beyond a single teleconsultation, TeleCare is built as a complete ecosystem for digital healthcare.  

The platform supports end‑to‑end appointment management, rich patient records, doctor management, and can be extended with analytics and operational dashboards.  

It’s designed to fit into real clinical workflows, while giving healthcare organizations the flexibility to scale and customize the solution to their own needs and regulations.”

---

### 9. Closing (company capability focus)

“With deep healthcare expertise and years of experience building secure, scalable medical platforms, Bacancy helps healthcare organizations bring powerful digital solutions to life.  

If you’re looking to build the next generation of telemedicine or broader healthcare platforms, our team can help turn your vision into a working product that patients and providers actually enjoy using.”
